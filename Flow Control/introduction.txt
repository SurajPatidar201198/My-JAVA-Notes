===========================================================================
Flow Control
===========================================================================

Flow control describes the order in which the Statements will be executed at runtime.
It is of 3 type of flow control statment:

----------------------------------------
Selection Statement
----------------------------------------
1) if-else
2) switch

-----------------------------------------
Iterative Statement
-----------------------------------------
1)while()
2)do-while()
3)for()
4)for-each

-----------------------------------------
Transfer Statement
-----------------------------------------
1)break
2)continue
3)return
4)try-catch-finally
5)assert
